Frequently Asked Questions About Our Lab!



Q1: Is this lab difficult?

A1: Much of the material we cover in class does require a high leavel of mathmatical understanding. Most of our work will also require significant patience, as you will naturally make many mistakes.
But there are always students and teachers to help you if you ever fall behind in your assignments!



Q2: What would you gain out of this lab?

A2: You will gain what you put into our lab, many of the skills you will gain include Object-Oriented Programming,
Game Development using Unity, Web Development on HTML
& Leadership Skills through Buisness Professionals of America.



Q3: What careers are associated with this lab?

A3: There are many IT careers that involve our lab, the most significant
of these include Software Developer, Software Engineer, Programmer,
Junior Developer & Web Developer



Q4: Who is eligible to join this lab?

A4: Any upcoming Sophomore can join our lab, our Career Center offers
1-year IT courses for seniors as well. We offer open houses for
Freshman & middle school students.



Q5: What is the work environment like?

A5: Our lab has a very unique and diverse work environment.
We often discuss topics with each other during lab and play icebreakers
to better understand each other. We get along very well
and we enjoy doing partner projects and group competitions.



Q6: What are some certificates students will gain?

A6: In this lab, student will gain several important,
industry-grade certifications that will make them stand out on their
resume. Junior year, we will obtain Micrososft Excel Office 2019
Associates Certificates, with Expert Certificates offered the following year.
We will also get the Unity Certified Programmer Basic Certificate.
We also have Certiport IT Skills Certificates offered during the year for students to gain.
